What is Truth & Reconcilation?

On June 26, 2018 the Alachua County Board of County Commissioners (BoCC) began discussion of a process for Truth and Reconciliation. The process was prompted by research completed by the County Historical Commission in response to the national Equal Justice Initiative lynching research including Alachua County. Local research confirmed the EJI list and more (since EJI begins at end of Reconstruction in 1877) documenting 43 lynchings that occurred between 1867 and 1926.
